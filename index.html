Test Information

The test is comprised of 15 problems to be done in 45 minutes (no calculator, only paper and pencil). It is meant to be a fun experience, so just sit back and enjoy the problems. The best part is that you don't have to show your work! Additionally, participation will give you some extra credit points in your math class.
